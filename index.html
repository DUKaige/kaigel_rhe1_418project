Features


GUI

Meeowth is a math tool that depends completely on graphical operations. All the modules in the app are always visible. You never need code.


Drag and Connect

Meeowth allows users to simply connect and state the relationship between modules by dragging and connecting.


Powerful Algebra

Meeowth is capable of calculation, polynomial solving, differentiation, simplification and factorization.


Powerful Graphics

With simple operations, different functions can be plotted on the same graph with different color.


Store your work

Meeowth saves users' works and allows users to access their previous works.


Specially designed keyboard

Meeowth has its own nicely designed keyboard, including all the necessary basic math functions.
